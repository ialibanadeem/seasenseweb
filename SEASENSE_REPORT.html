Fleet Status Logic

  • Idle: Speed ≤ 2 knots AND signal received within 5 minutes.
  • Offline: No GPS signals received for over 5 minutes.

04 Non-Technical Overview
🌊 What is SeaSense AI?

SeaSense AI is like an "Air Traffic Control" system for boats. It allows fleet owners to see exactly where every vessel is, how it's behaving, and whether it's safe to be out on the water.

📡 Real-Time Updates

The system uses "WebSockets," which is like a permanent open phone call between the boat and your screen. Icons move the second the boat moves.

📏 Accurate Distance

Since the Earth is round, we use the "Haversine Formula" to measure travel distance perfectly, accounting for the curvature of the planet.
